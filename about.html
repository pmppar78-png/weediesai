About WeediesAI

Our Mission

WeediesAI was created with a simple goal: to provide cannabis enthusiasts with trustworthy, educational information and intelligent tools that make navigating the cannabis landscape easier and safer.

In an industry often clouded by misinformation, confusing laws, and overwhelming choices, we believe everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, and unbiased information. WeediesAI combines curated educational content with AI-powered assistance to help adults make informed decisions about cannabis — always within the bounds of the law.

What We Do

WeediesAI is an educational platform and resource hub. We provide:

  • AI-Powered Tools: Our Strain Finder AI helps you discover strains based on your preferences. Our Grower AI offers cultivation guidance for states where home growing is legal. Our Munchies AI helps satisfy cravings when you need inspiration.
  • Educational Content: In-depth articles about cannabis science, terpenes, cannabinoids, responsible use, and more.
  • Legal Resources: State-by-state information to help you understand cannabis laws in your jurisdiction.
  • Curated Partner Links: Connections to licensed dispensaries, reputable retailers, and trusted service providers.

What We Don't Do

WeediesAI does not sell cannabis. We do not ship, distribute, or facilitate the sale of cannabis products. We are not a dispensary, and we are not affiliated with any cannabis growing or sales operations. Our role is purely educational and informational.

We also do not provide medical advice. While we share general information about cannabis, we never claim that cannabis treats, cures, or prevents any medical condition. For health-related questions, always consult a licensed healthcare professional.

Our Values

  • Education First: We prioritize accurate, well-researched information over hype or marketing claims.
  • Compliance: We respect the law and encourage our users to do the same. Cannabis is only legal in certain jurisdictions, and we always emphasize "where legal" qualifiers.
  • Responsibility: We promote responsible consumption, safe transportation options, and awareness of the risks associated with cannabis use.
  • Transparency: We clearly disclose affiliate relationships and never compromise editorial integrity for commercial gain.
  • Adults Only: WeediesAI is strictly for users aged 21 and older.

Our Commitment to Safety

Cannabis can be enjoyed responsibly by adults in legal jurisdictions, but it's not without risks. We encourage users to:

  • Never drive or operate machinery while impaired
  • Start with low doses and go slow, especially with edibles
  • Store cannabis safely away from children and pets
  • Know your limits and consume in moderation
  • Understand your local laws before purchasing or growing
