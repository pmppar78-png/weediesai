How Our Dispensary Finder Works

WeediesAI connects you with established cannabis directory services that aggregate information about licensed dispensaries in legal states. We don't maintain our own dispensary listings — instead, we partner with industry-leading platforms like Weedmaps, Leafly, and Dutchie to provide you with comprehensive, up-to-date information.

Why Use Directory Partners?

  • Real-time menus and pricing from dispensaries
  • Verified customer reviews and ratings
  • Information about licensing status
  • Online ordering and delivery options where available
  • Deals and promotions from local dispensaries

Tips for Choosing a Dispensary

  • Verify the dispensary is state-licensed (check your state's regulatory website)
  • Read customer reviews for service quality and product selection
  • Compare prices across multiple dispensaries
  • Ask budtenders questions — they're there to help
  • Start with small quantities when trying new products
