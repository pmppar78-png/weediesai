Laws by State (High-Level Overview)

Cannabis laws change frequently. This summary is for general education only and may not be current. Always verify with official state resources before acting.

Fully Legal (Adult-Use & Medical)

Examples include CA, CO, WA, OR, NV, MI, IL, NY, NJ, MA, and others. Adult-use possession limits and home grow rules vary.

Medical-Only

Some states allow medical use with a qualifying condition and card but prohibit or limit adult-use and home cultivation.

Limited or No Legalization

In some states and countries, cannabis remains illegal or highly restricted. WEEDIES AI will not encourage illegal activity.

Tip: Ask Strain Finder AI or Grower AI to summarize your state’s rules. Always confirm with official state websites.

Meet Munchies AI — Your High-Vibe Food Oracle

Whether you're lightly buzzed, melting into the couch, or "okay, somebody please strap me to the Earth" levels of baked, Munchies AI knows exactly what's going to taste incredible right now.

This is your personal flavor concierge, your late-night co-pilot, your "I got you" food expert that never judges and always delivers. Whether you're a bro, a girl, non-binary, or anything in between, if you're hungry and high, you're home here.

Tell Munchies AI:

  • Where you are
  • What kind of high you're riding
  • What you're in the mood for
  • What strain you smoked or ate
  • How hungry you actually are (light snack, real meal, full feast)

Munchies AI takes all of that and builds a recommendation that fits your vibe, your appetite, and your location.

What Munchies AI Can Do for You

Personalized food magic:

  • Suggest snacks and meals that taste amazing with the kind of high you're on
  • Help you choose between junk food, comfort food, healthier options, or a little of everything
  • Match flavors to your mood and your strain's general effects (chill, giggly, sleepy, creative, social, etc.)
  • Give you simple, low-effort munchie ideas you can throw together in a few minutes

Delivery, drive-thru, and late-night options:

  • Help you think through nearby fast food, diners, or local spots that usually stay open late
  • Suggest using delivery apps (like major food and convenience delivery services) when you don't want to leave the couch
  • Steer you toward easy, realistic options based on the kind of food you're craving

Easy "stoner chef" creations:

  • Offer fun, simple snack and meal ideas with very few steps
  • Focus on things that are hard to mess up when you're high
  • Give you flavor combo ideas (sweet + salty, spicy + creamy, hot + cold) that hit even harder when the munchies kick in

When You're Too High

Munchies AI isn't just about going harder on snacks — it can also be your calm, grounded voice when you overdid it.

If you're feeling too high, it can:

  • Suggest light snacks and drinks that many people find grounding and easier on the stomach
  • Talk about foods and beverages that may help you feel more stable and comfortable (like gentle citrus, light carbs, water, herbal teas, or small salty snacks)
  • Help you focus on hydration, balance, and simple comfort foods instead of going overboard
  • Offer soothing, reassuring guidance and remind you that the feeling is temporary

It won't give medical advice, but it will do its best to point you toward choices that are calming, simple, and easy on both your body and your brain.

Vibe Modes Munchies AI Handles

You can ask Munchies AI things like:

  • "Give me the perfect lazy snack for this couch-lock."
  • "I'm on a giggly sativa and want something sweet and ridiculous."
  • "I want a real meal, not just chips. What should I get?"
  • "What's a good food when I'm this high but don't want to feel worse later?"
  • "Help, I think I'm too high. What should I eat or drink to feel more grounded?"

Personality and tone

Munchies AI should feel like:

  • A chill friend who knows food
  • A private chef who understands cravings
  • A flavor nerd who gets how tastes and textures hit when you're high
  • A calm presence when you need to slow down and take care of yourself

No cringe, no judgement — just genuinely helpful, fun, and steady.
