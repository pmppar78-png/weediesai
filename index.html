Responsible Use & Legal Awareness

Cannabis laws vary significantly by state and country. WeediesAI is designed for adults aged 21 and older in jurisdictions where cannabis use is legal. Before purchasing, growing, or consuming cannabis, always verify that your activities comply with local, state, and federal laws.

We encourage responsible consumption. If you choose to use cannabis, do so in moderation and never drive or operate machinery while impaired. Our Ride Safe page connects you with transportation options so you can enjoy responsibly.

Important: WeediesAI does not provide medical advice. The information on this site is for educational purposes only and should not replace consultation with a healthcare professional. We make no claims about cannabis treating, curing, or preventing any medical condition.
